About Me

My name is Kambilinachukwu Valencia Udeani, but you can call me kamby. I’m from Enugu State (042), Ezeagu LGA, and the first of five girls raised by two incredible parents. I studied Information Science and Media Studies at Pan-Atlantic University, where I began to explore my interests across tech, business, and media. For a long time, I felt like I had to choose between these paths, but I’ve come to realize that I don’t. I’m building a life that intentionally blends all three. I’m naturally curious—I enjoy podcasts, films, fashion, and anything that feels like an adventure. More than anything, I’m driven by God and a strong sense of purpose in what I’m called to build. I’m currently learning web development, not just to code, but to truly understand how things work and to move closer to my goal of becoming a tech startup founder. I want to build meaningful solutions, push the boundaries of what’s possible, and create impact. At the core of it all, I’m driven by a desire to make my parents proud, achieve financial freedom, and give back in a way that truly matters.
